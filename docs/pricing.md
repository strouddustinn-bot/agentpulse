# Pricing

Simple beta pricing for Linux server monitoring with policy-based remediation.

**All listed prices are in Canadian dollars (CAD).** Founding pricing locks the
rate below for life once fleet self-serve ships. Nothing below is an instant
self-serve multi-host product today.

> **Current boundary:** the local agent is real and installable as a controlled
> pilot on a host you approve. Multi-server fleet control, secure account claim,
> and public self-serve install are not released. Charging for undeliverable
> fleet capacity is worse than a waitlist — so Pro and Business are
> **Founding Customer reserves**, not checkout.

| Plan | Founding price | Scope today | When it fully unlocks |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | C$29/mo | 1 server pilot (manual onboarding) | Single-host pilot after you request access |
| **Pro — recommended** | **C$99/mo** | Founding reserve for up to 5 servers | Fleet control plane target: **Q3** |

**Most founders reserve Pro.** One bad night, one churn-causing outage, or one
oversized monitoring bill already costs more than C$99 — and founding customers
lock that beta rate for life.

## Why not buy online yet?

Self-serve checkout without a proven path from payment → account → working
install instructions creates refund risk and public “vaporware tier” narrative.
Fleet control plane ships **Q3**. Founding customers lock beta pricing for life
and validate demand before that capacity is charged as available.

Today you can:

1. **Reserve** Pro or Business founding pricing (no charge at reserve).
2. **Request a 1-server Starter pilot** if you want a controlled manual install
   on a non-critical host after we confirm fit.
3. Prefer email first? Write
   [support@agentpulse.ca](mailto:support@agentpulse.ca?subject=AgentPulse%20founding%20reserve).

## Founding guarantee (when billing starts)

When a reserved plan is activated and billed, if AgentPulse does not catch or
reduce at least one repeat operational incident in the first 30 days of paid
service, cancel and pay nothing for the next month.

## Cancellation

After paid service starts, cancel by emailing
[support@agentpulse.ca](mailto:support@agentpulse.ca).

## Best fit

AgentPulse is strongest today for founders and small teams running Linux
servers on providers like Hetzner, DigitalOcean, Linode, Vultr, AWS Lightsail,
or small EC2 fleets. Multi-host fleet management is the Q3 control-plane track.

## What counts as a server?

A server is one Linux machine or VPS running the AgentPulse agent. Containers on
the same host do not count separately during the beta.

## Need something custom?

Email [support@agentpulse.ca](mailto:support@agentpulse.ca?subject=AgentPulse%20Business%20founding%20reserve)
with the number of servers, the stack you run, and the incidents you want
remediated.
